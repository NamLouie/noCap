USA Peer to Peer

The mentoring program at the University of San Agustin aims to leverage the existing knowledge, skills, and experience of junior and senior BSCS and BSIT students to support and guide freshman and sophomore students in these programs.

Benefits of Peer to Peer Mentoring:

  • Connect with experienced mentors in your field.
  • Engage in collaborative learning with peers.
  • Receive personalized academic support and guidance.
